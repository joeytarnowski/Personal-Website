About Me

My interests include machine learning and cybersecurity, and my most extensive projects have been in reinforcement learning. I am most proficient in Python, but I also have experience in working with HTML/CSS, Javascript, Java, C#, SQL, and VBA for Microsoft Office.

I have also worked as debate coach at both the high school and college levels for several years, which has helped me develop strong communication, research, and leadership skills.
